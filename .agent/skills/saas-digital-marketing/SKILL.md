# SKILL: SaaS Digital Marketing (The Executor)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Filosofía**: "Content is King, Distribution is Queen."
> This skill executes the strategy defined by `saas-marketing`.

## Core Tactics

| Tactic | Focus | Deliverable |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| **SEO** | Organic Traffic | Keyword List, Blog Outlines, Meta Tags. |
| **Ads (PPC)** | Immediate Traffic | Ad Copy (Headlines, Descriptions), Targeting. |
| **Email** | Retention/Nurture | Drip Sequences (Welcome, Churn Recovery). |
| **Social** | Brand Awareness | Content Calendar (Posts/Tweets). |

## Critical Patterns

### 1. SEO Intent Mapping
*   **Informational**: "How to manage dental patients" -> **Blog Post**.
*   **Transactional**: "Best dental CRM software" -> **Landing Page / Pricing**.

### 2. Ad Copy Frameworks
*   **AIDA**: Attention, Interest, Desire, Action.
*   **PAS**: Problem, Agitation, Solution. (Effective for B2B).
*   **Hook-Story-Offer**: Good for video/social.

### 3. Email Drip Sequence Structure
*   **Day 0**: Welcome + Value (No hard sell).
*   **Day 2**: Tip #1 (Solve a small problem).
*   **Day 4**: Case Study (Social Proof).
*   **Day 7**: Soft Sell (Upgrade/Demo).

## Anti-Patterns

*   ❌ **Buying Lists**: Never buy email lists. It ruins domain reputation.
*   ❌ **Clickbait**: Writing headers that deliver no value.
*   ❌ **One-Channel Dependency**: Relying 100% on Facebook Ads (Platform risk).
*   ❌ **Broken Links**: Sending traffic to 404 pages.

## Instructions for Agent

1.  **Ask for Channels**: "Are we focusing on LinkedIn (B2B) or Instagram (B2C)?"
2.  **Generate Assets**: Don't just say "write a blog post". Write the *Title, Slug, Keywords, and Outline*.
3.  **Write Copy**: Provide 3 variations of Ad Headlines to test (A/B testing).
